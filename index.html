Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility criteria for these internships?

+

Anyone with a basic understanding of programming concepts can apply for these internships. Students pursuing a degree in Computer Science, IT, or related fields are preferred. However, passion for learning is our primary criterion.

How is the internship conducted?

+

The internships are conducted completely online. You'll have access to learning materials, assignments, and mentorship through our virtual platform. Regular video sessions will be conducted for doubt-clearing and project discussions.

Will I receive a certificate after completion?

+

Yes, upon successful completion of the internship program and all assigned projects, you will receive a certificate of completion that highlights the skills you've acquired during the internship.

Is there any placement assistance after the internship?

+

Top-performing interns may receive recommendations and referrals to our partner companies. While we don't guarantee placements, our network can certainly provide valuable opportunities for deserving candidates.

What is the selection process for these internships?

+

The selection process typically involves an online application followed by a basic technical assessment to evaluate your understanding of fundamentals. Shortlisted candidates may go through a brief interview before final selection.

Can I apply for multiple internship programs simultaneously?

+

Yes, you can apply for multiple programs, but we recommend focusing on one program at a time for the best learning experience. If you're unsure which program to choose, you can mention your preferences in your application.
